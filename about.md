The Bulgarian C++ Working Group — formally the Technical Committee
"C++ and Programming Languages" — operates under the
[Bulgarian Standardization Institute (BDS)](https://www.bds-bg.org).
We serve as the national mirror body for
**ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 22/WG 21**, the international committee
responsible for the C++ programming language standard (ISO/IEC 14882).

## What We Do

- Review and vote on C++ standard proposals (papers) within the ISO process.
- Hold regular meetings to discuss upcoming standard features, language direction, and national positions.

## Meetings

Our meetings are open — anyone can participate. If you would like to give a talk on any
subject related to C++, contact one of the working group members and we will schedule it
based on interest, priority, and already scheduled talks.
